Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

GitaVerse is a simple daily reading app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data is used and how.

What We Collect

GitaVerse does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data only on your device, never on any server:

  • Your start date (to calculate which verse is shown today)
  • Cached verse data and AI insights (to enable offline use)

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser or app cache.

Third-Party Services

GitaVerse uses the following external services:

  • Vedic Scriptures API (vedicscriptures.github.io) — to fetch Bhagavad Gita verse data. No personal data is sent.
  • Anthropic Claude API — to generate AI insights for each verse. Only the verse text is sent; no personal information is transmitted.
  • Google Fonts — to load the Noto Sans Devanagari and Crimson Text fonts.

No Advertising

GitaVerse contains no advertisements and does not use any advertising networks or analytics trackers.

Children's Privacy

GitaVerse does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains only spiritual and educational content.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@dailygitaverse.netlify.app or via the GitHub repository.
